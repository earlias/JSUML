Magic: The Gathering

Introduction

Magic: The Gathering (MTG) is a collectible trading card game that was created by Richard Garfield. It was released in 1993 through Wizards of the Coast, the same company that released Dungeons and Dragons. Magic, for short, is played with two or more players across a variety of different formats that include different pools of the over twenty-thousand cards currently in circulation. There are various different types of cards, such as creatures, sorceries, and enchantments, that are all referred to as "spells." Players take turns casting spells at each other or onto a board, in order to win, typically by reducing their opponent(s) to 0 life points. The different types of cards come across 5 different colors; blue (U), green (G), white (W), black (B), and red (R).
